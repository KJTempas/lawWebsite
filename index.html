Darryl Graves is a respected and professional attorney with 40 years of experience in the practice of law and is licensed in Kansas, Arkansas and Colorado. Darryl practices primarily in the areas of real estate law, wills and trusts, business and corporate formation, landlord and tenant disputes, and debt collection. In addition to his vast legal experience, Darryl is a licensed Kansas real estate broker and a former insurance adjuster.

Mike Jilka is a 1988 graduate of the University of Kansas Law School. He served judicial clerkships with the Kansas Court of Appeals and U.S. District Judge Monti Belot, and also served as an Assistant Attorney General. Mike has been in private practice since 1994. He is licensed in Kansas and Missouri.
